03 — FAQ

Real Questions, Clear Answers

Here’s what students and institutions ask us most often — and how scholarlySPI helps.

“Is ScholarlySPI free to use?”

Yes. Students can explore scholarships, apply, and track their progress without any cost.

“Can I apply to multiple scholarships at once?”

Absolutely. Upload your documents once and reuse them across multiple applications seamlessly.

“How secure is my data?”

Your information is encrypted and stored with strict privacy controls. You’re always in control.

“Do I need to complete my profile before applying?”

We recommend it. A complete profile helps us match you with the most relevant scholarships and improves your chances of success.

“Can institutions customize their application forms?”

Yes. Institutions can tailor forms, eligibility filters, and review workflows to fit their unique requirements.

“What kind of support does ScholarlySPI offer?”

We offer call, email support and access to mentors who can guide students through the application process.
